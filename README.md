# RammyWhammy-30Days

A story-driven browser game where you live 30 consecutive days of Ramadan — the holy month where Muslims fast and deepen their connection to Islam — through the eyes of Sarah, a student juggling faith, school, and family.

---

## 🎮 Game Access

**Step 1:** Click here → [**Play RammyWhammy**](https://shammmn.github.io/RammyWhammy-30Days/)  
**Step 2:** Start the game and begin Day 1 of Ramadan!

---

## 📖 Game Overview

You play as **Sarah**, a Muslim student navigating the challenges of Ramadan: keeping up her grades, maintaining her faith (Iman), managing her energy while fasting, and saving up for Eid. Every decision across 30 days matters — one wrong move could mean exhaustion, broke savings, or missing the scholarship of a lifetime.

---

## 🏫 The Academy Mandate

- **Weekday Mornings (Mon–Fri):** Sarah is locked into the Academy. You cannot visit the Market, Mosque, or Mall during this time.
- **The Friday Test:** Every Friday morning, the Academy option becomes a high-stakes test — your biggest opportunity to boost your **Grades** stat, but it drains a significant amount of **Energy**.

## 💼 The Work Mandate

- **Tuesday & Thursday Afternoons:** Sarah is required to work her shift. These time slots are locked — you cannot skip or substitute them for another activity.

---

## ⚡ Survival Vitals

Two stats you absolutely cannot ignore:

| Stat | Color | What Happens If It Runs Out |
|---|---|---|
| **Energy** | 🔵 Blue | Hits 0% → Sarah collapses from exhaustion → **Game Over instantly** |
| **Savings** | 🟢 Green | Runs out → Can't buy healthy food → **Energy drops faster** |

> 💡 Always check your Energy before choosing **"Intense Study"** or **"Double Shifts."**  
> 💡 You start with **$100** — budget carefully for Iftar meals and your Eid outfit.

---

## 📅 Special Events

### 🛍️ SuhoorFest *(Saturdays)*
Every **Saturday Evening**, visiting the Mall triggers **SuhoorFest** — the best way to max out your **Social** stat and spend quality time with friends.

### 🤲 Mom's Blessing *(Day 28)*
Visit **Home on Day 28** with your **Iman at 70% or higher** and Mom rewards you with an **$80 bonus**.

---

## 🏆 Scholarship Requirements

To win the scholarship and receive the **Professor's compliment at the Day 30 party**, you must hit all three targets:

| # | Requirement | Target |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 📚 **Grades** | 85% or higher |
| 2 | 🕌 **Iman** | 80% or higher |
| 3 | 👗 **The Designer Abaya** | $350 saved by end of Day 30 |

> All three conditions must be met. Miss even one and the scholarship slips away.

---

## 🌙 Tips for New Players

- **Don't skip the Mosque** — it's your primary way to build Iman, which is easy to neglect when school and work feel urgent.
- **Saturday Malls = SuhoorFest** — never waste a Saturday if your Social stat is low.
- **Watch Friday Energy** — the Friday Test is worth it, but don't go in already drained.
- **Plan for Day 28** — make sure your Iman is above 70% before then to secure Mom's $80 bonus, which meaningfully helps toward the $350 Abaya goal.
